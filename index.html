About the app

A forecasting layer between the Sun and the systems that depend on it

Solar flares and coronal mass ejections can disrupt satellite links, GPS accuracy, HF radio, and power grids within minutes of eruption. Most existing tools report flares after they've already happened — HELIOS is built to forecast them ahead of time.

The system ingests continuous X-ray flux data, solar magnetogram imagery, and sunspot activity, and runs it through a trained model that estimates the likely flare class, expected timing, and probability of an Earth-directed CME — surfaced through a live dashboard and automated alerts.

Built as part of an ISRO hackathon submission, HELIOS is designed to eventually plug into data from India's Aditya-L1 solar observation mission alongside existing GOES and SDO feeds.

Key features

Everything a mission control screen needs, nothing it doesn't

Built around one job — turn raw solar data into a decision someone can act on in time.

☀

Real-time flux monitoring

Streams GOES X-ray flux data continuously and flags deviations from baseline before they cross a flare threshold.

◈

ML-based class prediction

Estimates upcoming flare class (A through X) and peak timing using a model trained on historical active-region behaviour.

⤳

CME trajectory estimation

Flags when a coronal mass ejection is likely to be Earth-directed, and gives a rough arrival window.

⚠

Operator alerting

Pushes threshold-based alerts to satellite, grid, and aviation operators so response time isn't spent finding the data first.

▤

Active region tracking

Watches individual sunspot regions over their rotation across the solar disk and ranks them by eruption risk.

▦

Historical flare archive

Searchable record of past events by class, region, and date — for validating predictions against what actually happened.

How it works

From raw signal to a usable warning

Four stages, running continuously, from data ingestion to an alert reaching someone's screen.

01

Ingest

Pulls live X-ray flux, magnetogram, and sunspot data from GOES, SDO, and Aditya-L1 feeds.

02

Preprocess

Cleans and aligns time-series and image data, isolating active regions worth tracking.

03

Predict

Model scores each active region for flare class, peak timing, and CME likelihood.

04

Alert

Crosses a confidence threshold → dashboard updates and operators are notified automatically.
